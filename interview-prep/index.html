What is the difference between IDS and IPS?

An IDS (Intrusion Detection System) monitors and alerts on suspicious traffic. An IPS (Intrusion Prevention System) actively blocks it inline. The key distinction is passive observation vs active intervention...

Describe the Cyber Kill Chain phases.

Reconnaissance → Weaponization → Delivery → Exploitation → Installation → C2 → Actions on Objectives. In practice, defenders focus on breaking the chain as early as possible...
