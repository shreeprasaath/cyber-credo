Cyber Credo

A field notebook from the blue team trenches.

Hi — I'm a cybersecurity practitioner working in blue team operations. Cyber Credo is where I document what I learn, tools I actually use, and experiences from the field.

This isn't a polished marketing blog. It's a field notebook — written for people who are in the same place I was: trying to figure out how real infosec work looks before you have the fancy title to go with it.

What I cover

Blue team operations, SIEM tuning, threat hunting, home lab builds, cert prep, and the occasional deep dive into something that caught my attention during an investigation. The goal is to write the posts I wish had existed when I was starting out.
